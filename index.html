The Process
No discretion.
Just three rules.

15M Sweep

Wait for price to breach a key 15M level inside the London or NY killzone. The sweep triggers the setup — nothing before it counts.

A Fair Value Gap must form on the reversal. Enter on retest. SL fixed at the sweep candle — never moved. TP1 at 2R, TP2 at 3R.
